# Hey, I'm Mário 👋

I'm a 35-year-old data & AI leader based in **Lisbon, Portugal**. I currently work as **Head of Data & AI at [QuintoAndar](https://www.quintoandar.com.br/)**, where I lead teams building the data and AI foundation for one of Latin America's largest real estate platforms.

My career has been shaped by a deep belief that good data engineering is the backbone of great products — and that the best teams are built on trust, curiosity, and a bias for action.
